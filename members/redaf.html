Reda Fatima

Research Assistant

Reda Fatima is a Software Engineering student at the University of Greenwich with experience in software development, data engineering, and cybersecurity. She has worked at Citi Bank in both the Trade Compliance and Core Banking teams, where she developed and updated ETL pipelines, performed data reconciliation for system migration, and worked with technologies such as Java, SQL, Hive, and Hadoop. At university, she has contributed to several technical projects, including a machine learning model for detecting fraud using FCA synthetic data, a leaf disease detection system using CNNs, a transport mapping application using graph algorithms, and a peer-to-peer WhatsApp simulator in Java. She has also held leadership roles, including President of the Computer Society and Programme Representative.

Her research interests and skills include:

  • cyber security
  • cryptography
  • quantitative finance
  • artificial intelligence
  • big data engineering
