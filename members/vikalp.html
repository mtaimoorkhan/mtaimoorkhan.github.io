Vikalp Jaiswal

Research Assistant in Lex Ex Machina

Vikalp Jaiswal is a master’s student of Computer Forensics and Cyber Security at the University of Greenwich, London and a graduate in bachelor’s of technology in computer Science from Amity University, India. Vikalp was a Software Engineer in the Application Development and Support practice of Sopra Steria India. He has been part of Oppo India manufacturing private. ltd. where he managed MES system, one of the crown jewels of Enterprise Resource Planning. As an Intern in Oracle India, he played key role in UPI integration to the banking system. In his career spanning over 4 years he has rich work experience across domains like Banking Solutions, Application Support & Development, Incident & Problem Management, Manufacturing ERP and Law. Vikalp is currently supporting LETS Lab research on legal tech.

His research interests and skills include:

  • cyber security
  • computer forensics
  • application development
