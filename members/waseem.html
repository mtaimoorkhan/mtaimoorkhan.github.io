Muhammad Waseem, Dr.

Senior Research Fellow

Muhammad Waseem is a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Jyväskylä, Finland. Before that, he completed his Ph.D. from the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, China in December 2021. During his Ph.D. studies, he has published 7 peer-reviewed journal and conference papers as the first author in leading software engineering venues such as JSS, ICSE-SEIP, ICSOC, EASE, and APSEC.

His research interests and skills include:

  • Quantum Computing
  • Security architecture
  • Security analysis
